Every field must not have white spaces and they must begin with an alphanumeric character.


I field: name of character
	e.g. greater-thanSign, plusMinus


II field: for internal reverse functions of codex


III field: exadecimal or decimal value


IV field: xmlux command


V filed: LaTeX equivalent command 
	(it needs for xmlux future automatic conversion from *.lmx to *.tex)


VI filed: LaTeX command package: <default> if command is LaTeX built-in.
	(it needs for xmlux future automatic conversion from *.lmx to *.tex)
